About us

Hello Dayton flyers, we're UD Dining Services, your on campus dining service. We know you’re always busy. No time for cooking. So let us take care of you meals, we’re really good at it, we promise! The University of Dayton Dining Services proudly supports the institution's commitment to healthy living, serving a variety of locally grown and sourced as well as fair trade products throughout campus dining facilities!
